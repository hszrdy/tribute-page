Gabe Newell

Gabe Newell is a important and influential figure in the realm of video games.

He founded VALVe, which has created and published many legendary games such as:

  • Half-Life Series
  • Portal 1 & 2
  • Team Fortress 2
  • and many more...

It is also noteworthy that VALVe also created and owns Steam, which is one of the largest and popular digital platform for games

VALVe has also created hardware such as the Steam Deck
